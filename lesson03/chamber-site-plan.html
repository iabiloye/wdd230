Purpose

The Calabar Chamber of Commerce serves as a driving force for regional economic growth, a facilitator of leadership initiatives, and a dedicated advocate for the local community in Calabar, Nigeria. Our mission is to foster business development by offering comprehensive member services, actively contributing to economic and community progress, and advocating for public policies that strike a harmonious balance between fostering economic prosperity and addressing community needs. Through our multifaceted approach, we aim to create a vibrant and sustainable business environment that contributes to the overall well-being of Calabar and its residents.
